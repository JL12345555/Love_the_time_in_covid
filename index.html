Covid-19 (Daily Reported case,Hospitalization rate, ICU rate) visualization in Ottawa From 2020-2021

***Warning: This is not interactive

written sections

        The motivation of our webpage is help Ottawa citizen understand the trend of COVID-19 from 2020-2021; how does lockdown,mandate and vaccines effects trend of COVID-19; the hospitalization rate and icu proportion.
        We used COVID-19 Daily cases, Cases in Hospital and Cases in ICU from January, 2020 to December, 2021. Our data are from Open Ottawa, all the data are quantity data.
        We determined the time range are 2020-2021, using 3 line chart instead of bar chart to show how daily number effects numbers of hospital and icu; determined the independent variable is date instead of month.

Design Process:
We chose the sketch 1 first, but due to the amount of domain, we decided changed to line chart which is sketch 3.
